What is Country music?

Country music (sometimes called Country & Western) is a form of music that has been enjoyed by people all over the United States for decades. Country music has its roots and beginnings in folk music. Modern-day Country music started in the years after World War II. The Sons of the Pioneers and The Carter Family performed old and new songs, in a way that reminded people of singalongs and jamborees of the past.
