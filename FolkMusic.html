What is Folk music?

Folk music is music that is played or sung by unprofessional musicians. It is traditional music that people learn by listening to other people playing it and then copying them. In many parts of the world nearly all music is folk music. The term “folk music” is usually used for European and American music which is part of an oral tradition.
